Vegeta

Background

Vegeta IV is the Prince of All Saiyans. His father, King Vegeta, led a rebellion against Frieza prior to his and his planet's destruction. Vegeta came to Earth with fellow saiyan Nappa to look for the Dragon Balls, but Goku, Gohan,and Krillin stopped them. Several years and battles later, Vegeta finally changed when he married one of Goku's oldest friends, Bulma, and had a son.

Abilities

  1. Super Saiyan- Not wanting Goku to leave him in the dust, Vegeta transformed into a Super Saiyan.
  2. Super Saiyan 2-Vegeta trains 7 years after the defeat of Cell to achieve this form.
  3. Super Saiyan God- Unlike Goku, Vegeta reaches this form without the Super Saiyan God Ritual.
  4. Super Saiyan God Super Saiyan (Blue) Vegeta achieves this form while training alongside Goku.
  5. Super Saiyan God Super Saiyan Evolution- Vegeta breaks through his limits of Blue to achieve greater power.
  6. Ultra Ego- Vegeta obtains God of Destruction powers and reaches this form.

Techniques

  1. Galick Gun- similar to the Kamehameha, but not quite as powerful.
  2. Big Bang Attack- Creates a giant explosion on impact.
  3. Final Flash-Focuses user's energy into a massive energy wave
  4. Hakai- Destruction Energy that completely erases what it destroys.
