Goku

Background

Goku is a Saiyan who was born on Planet Vegeta as Kakarot just days before the evil overlord Frieza blew it up. To save him, Bardock, Goku's father and Gine, Goku's mother, sent him to Earth so he could survive. Goku was found by an old and kind man, Gohan, who raised little Kakarot and renamed him Goku. Goku was really tough on his grandpa Gohan, until one day he fell into a ravine and severely injured his head. After he recovered, Goku became a loving and caring young boy and trained under the likes of Master Roshi, Korin, Kami, and many others.

Abilities

  1. Super Saiyan-a powerup that makes the hair spike up and turn blonde, and makes the eyes turn emerald. Obtained through extreme rage or the pain of loss, features a 50x increase in power/abilities.
  2. Super Saiyan 2- next level of super saiyan with sharpened facial features and enhanced spiked hair,with a lightning-like aura and a 100x multiplier, or 50x stronger than super saiyan.
  3. Super Saiyan 3- a 400x multiplier from base form, it features long, spiky hair and the disappearance of the eyebrows.
  4. Super Saiyan God- originally requiring 5 pure hearted Saiyans transferring their super saiyan power into the host, Goku trained and learned to use this power at will. featuring red hair, red eyes, a fiery aura and a thinner body, Super Sauyan God is literally Goku obtaining the power of a god and an 80,000 multiplier from base.
  5. Super Saiyan God Super Saiyan (Super Saiyan Blue)- The power beyond that of a Super Saiyan God, Super Saiyan Blue is the equivalent of going super saiyan while in Super Saiyan God form. Featuring a blue aura and blue spiked hair similar to super saiyan, this form boasts a 4,000,000,000x multiplier from base!
    Techniques
  1. Kamehameha- Goku's signature technique
  2. Spirit Bomb- host gathers energy from all living things and forms a massive ball of energy capable of ultimate damage
  3. Kaioken- a power multiplier that boosts the user's abilities, currently up to x20, at the cost of a strain on the user's body unless properly conditioned. Goku can also stack this on top of Super Saiyan Blue
  4. Ultra Instinct- the ultimate technique that even the gods have a hard time obtaining. The user's body moves on it's own instinct, but it requires a calm heart and mind and the proper training to master it. Goku can call on this form at will after intense disciplinary training.
